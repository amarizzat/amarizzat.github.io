Educational Background

--> --> -->
My first primary school that I went to is Sekolah Kebangsaan Guar Chempedak. The duration I was there for is 4 years until I switched school. The reason I switched to a new primary school is because I moved to a new house. My second primary school is Sekolah Kebangsaan Haji Hussin Dol. I finished my primary education there for 2 years.

I entered my secondary school when I was 13 years old. My secondary school is Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Guar Chempedak. I studied there for 5 years until I passed SPM.

To further my education to a higher level, for my diploma, I studied in Information Management at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Kampus Kedah. As of right now, I am now in Bachelor of Information Science (Hons) Records Management at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Kampus Segamat, Johor. I am now in my 3rd semester.
