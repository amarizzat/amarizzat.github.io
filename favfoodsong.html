Favourite Food

My favourite food has to be chicken chop. Chicken chop is a dish that is prepared with boneless chicken meat. This dish is popular in almost all culinary ethnicities owing to its luscious taste and ease of making. It is usually prepared with minced meat and served with various accompaniments. Seasonings and binding agents plays an important roll in the preparation of the chicken chop recipe.
