My Interests

One of my main interests is in technology . As to why I love technology, is because it constantly changes the world around us. By making every day tasks easier technology opens up so many other options that you might have been missing out in life.

I have an example that I personally experienced. Before smartphones we were tracking nutrition and calorie intake manually. Most of the time we couldn’t get the precise numbers for every food so it was a bit frustrating and time consuming. Nowdays people just use My Fitness Pall and enter the meal they just had. The app then calculates the exact number of calories they eaten and can eat for the rest of the day.
